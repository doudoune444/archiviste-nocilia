## Reviewer note
Verdict APPROVE because no HIGH/security finding and all 15 ACs are met or precedent-consistent. The single MED (`; charset=utf-8` on the 200 path) is inherited byte-for-byte from the already-merged OBS-001 `stats.rs` / OBS-002 `status.rs` pattern — fixing it here in isolation would diverge `/v1/quality` from its siblings. Recommend a follow-up ticket to add `application/json; charset=utf-8` to all three public-API JSON success paths uniformly, rather than blocking OBS-004.
